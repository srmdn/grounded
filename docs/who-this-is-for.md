# Who This Is For

Grounded is opinionated.

It is not for everyone.

## Good Fit

Grounded is for people who:

- build mostly alone
- have limited budget
- use AI tools but feel scattered
- want a disciplined path from idea to shipped app
- care more about usefulness than startup theater

It is especially useful if you keep hitting the same pattern:

- excitement at the start
- fast scaffolding
- feature expansion
- uncertainty about what matters
- stalled momentum or shallow progress

## Not a Good Fit

Grounded is probably not for people who:

- want a full startup course
- need a marketing or growth framework
- already have a mature team process
- mainly want inspiration, not operational structure
- prefer building large systems before validating smaller ones

## Why This Boundary Matters

Many projects fail because the builder tries to serve everyone:

- founders
- developers
- agencies
- content creators
- hobbyists

Grounded should stay narrow enough to stay sharp.

It is for solo builders who need better judgment under constraints.
