# Use Grounded With AI

Grounded is not an AI product by itself.

Today, it is a structured framework that works well with AI tools such as Codex, Claude, ChatGPT, or any other agent/chat interface.

## What AI Is Doing

When you use Grounded with AI:

- Grounded provides the method
- AI helps apply the method to your specific situation
- you still make the final judgment

Think of it as:

- Grounded = structure
- AI = reasoning partner
- you = decision maker

## Best Moments To Use It With AI

Use Grounded with AI when:

- your idea is still vague
- you have too many possible next steps
- you are unsure whether to simplify or expand
- you need to define a real milestone
- you just finished something and need to decide what comes next

## Simple Prompt Pattern

Use this shape:

```text
Use the Grounded approach on this situation.

Context:
[describe the project or problem]

Help me clarify:
- who this is for
- what painful problem it solves
- what the smallest useful version is
- what the next bottleneck is
- what the next sane step should be

Also tell me what is tempting but not necessary yet.
```

## Good AI Roles For Grounded

AI is useful for:

- clarifying vague thinking
- turning a messy idea into a spec-lite
- identifying weak milestones
- spotting overbuilding
- summarizing tradeoffs
- drafting project briefs, reviews, and checklists

## Bad AI Roles For Grounded

AI should not replace judgment.

Be careful when using AI for:

- deciding what matters without enough context
- expanding scope too early
- producing impressive-sounding plans with no real constraints
- confirming your bias instead of challenging it

## Practical Examples

### Example 1: Software project

Ask AI:

"Use Grounded on this project. I am torn between adding auth, improving onboarding, or fixing deploy friction. Tell me what the next bottleneck actually is."

### Example 2: Personal brand

Ask AI:

"Use Grounded on my personal brand. I have too many content ideas and no clear direction. Help me pick one audience, one message, and one repeatable format."

### Example 3: Small business

Ask AI:

"Use Grounded on this small business plan. Help me separate what is essential for the first real version from what is only nice to have."

## What Grounded Might Become Later

Later, Grounded may become a lightweight interface on top of an LLM.

That could look like:

- a guided web app
- a structured chat interface
- a CLI assistant
- reusable agent prompts and flows

But that is future direction.

Today, the simplest usable version is:

- read the docs
- use the templates
- bring the framework into your AI conversation
