# Manifest

Grounded exists because modern builders are drowning in output and starving for judgment.

This is true in software, but not only in software.

It is also true for:

- people building a personal brand
- people building a small business
- people packaging expertise
- people trying to turn learning into real progress
- people trying to move from scattered effort to deliberate execution

Grounded is for anyone building something real under constraints.

## The Core Problem

The problem is not lack of ideas.

The problem is:

- too many ideas
- too many tools
- too much motion
- too little prioritization
- too little clarity about what matters now

This creates a common modern failure mode:

People look productive, but they are not advancing meaningfully.

## What Grounded Believes

Grounded believes:

- clarity matters more than hype
- execution matters more than inspiration
- the next sane step matters more than the grand plan
- constraints are part of reality, not an inconvenience to ignore
- a useful thing is better than a dramatic one

## What Grounded Is

Grounded is:

- a decision system
- an anti-drift framework
- a practical method for choosing what to do next
- a structure that humans and AI tools can both use

## What Grounded Is Not

Grounded is not:

- an all-in-one business system
- a generic motivation product
- a promise that AI will solve your confusion for you
- a replacement for judgment

## How To Use Grounded Today

Use it when you are:

- clarifying an idea
- defining a milestone
- deciding whether to build, simplify, or wait
- reviewing what changed after shipping
- trying to separate what is useful from what is merely exciting

Today, Grounded is a docs-first system.
You use it with your own brain, with an AI assistant, or with a structured planning session.

Later, it may become a stronger interface layer.
But the method comes first.
