# Validation Checklist

- Main user and painful problem are still clear
- Current milestone solved a real bottleneck
- The shipped artifact is actually usable, not just complete
- At least one real workflow is easier than before
- Confusing parts are written down
- Wrong assumptions are written down
- Next bottleneck is identified
- Next step is chosen based on usefulness, not excitement
- Nice-to-have features are separated from actual blockers
- Decision to continue, pause, or change direction is explicit
